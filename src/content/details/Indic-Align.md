# IndicAlign

A diverse collection of Instruction and Toxic alignment datasets for 14 Indic Languages. The collection comprises of:

- **IndicAlign - Instruct**
  - Indic-ShareLlama
  - Dolly-T
  - OpenAssistant-T
  - WikiHow
  - IndoWordNet
  - Anudesh
  - Wiki-Conv
  - Wiki-Chat
- **IndicAlign - Toxic**
  - HHRLHF-T
  - Toxic-Matrix
